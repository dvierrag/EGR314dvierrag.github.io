## Overview
This bill of materials lists the components required for the ESP32 WiFi subsystem used in this project. The ESP32-S3-WROOM-1 module provides wireless connectivity and is powered through a regulated 3.3 V supply. Supporting components such as capacitors, resistors, and protection devices ensure stable operation and protect the circuit from overcurrent conditions.



## Bill of Materials (Example as Table)

**Table**: This bill of materials lists the electronic components required for the ESP32 WiFi subsystem used in this project.
