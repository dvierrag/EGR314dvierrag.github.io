## Conclusions

The power budget analysis confirms that the system operates within safe electrical limits and provides adequate power for all components. The voltage regulators supply stable 5 V and 3.3 V rails required by the ESP32 S3 WROOM 1 module and supporting circuitry. All subsystem current requirements were calculated and summed to determine the total system power consumption. The available power from the supply exceeds the total required current, which provides an appropriate safety margin for reliable operation. This margin also allows for small variations in component consumption and future expansion if additional peripherals are added. Based on these calculations, the power system design is sufficient to support the complete operation of the device without exceeding the supply limits.
